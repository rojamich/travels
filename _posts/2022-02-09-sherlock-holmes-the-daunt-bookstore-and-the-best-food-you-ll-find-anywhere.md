NEXT DOOR WAS A GEORGIAN (the country) Restaurant!! OMG I could not curb my enthusiasm. After I returned from a work trip to Tbilisi, Michael and I tried making Khinkali. They were good, but not Georgia good. After our couples massage (where a full-grown woman stood on me) we headed back to the restaurant and I scarfed down some Georgian food faster than I could take photos of it. I would go there every day if Michael didn’t insist we actually eat food from the country we are in!
