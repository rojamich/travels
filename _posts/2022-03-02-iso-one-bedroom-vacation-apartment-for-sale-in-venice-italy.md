Once we left the station, we were met with an odd sight. Tons and tons of people in strange costumes! You can see some of them in the photo on the left, by the dome! It turns out, we had arrived in the middle of a festival!

It is called the Venice Carnival and it’s a celebration of Venice’s victory over the Patriarch. It is celebrated for the last two weeks of March and everyone wears masks and costumes and throws confetti. We walked by people singing, dancing and gathering all over the squares!
