Happy New Year!! Its officially 2025 in Vietnam and we are starting the year off right with a tour of Ha Long Bay. One of the most recognizable sites of Vietnam. We decided to try a new breakfast spot this very early morning before our tour got started. Thankfully, there was a spot right around the corner from our hotel that was serving Bánh Cuốn: steamed rice rolls with ground pork, mushrooms, mint, cilantro, bean sprouts and shallots! 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782995943/20260101_070546_mrnjgp.jpg)

We hadn't tried this yet and it looked/smelled so good! The owners were very kind and taught us how to pronounce the different dishes - even recommending what was most loved. We were the only tourists this morning, surrounded by locals having breakfast before work! We were quickly seated and served. They delivered our food with this sauce... do we drink this? It looks like miso soup! 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782996211/20260101_070736_htixth.jpg)

We spotted other people eating and they were not drinking it. Thankfully, our food was delivered shortly thereafter and we were instructed to dip our food in it!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782994590/20260101_071153_jabjon.jpg)

This might be the best food I've ever had. Like my favorite meal of all time. Can I pronounce it? No. But I would eat it every day if I could!\
Walking back to our hotel, we saw a few different and interesting things being sold on the side of the road. 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782996822/20260101_072215_ybf9id.jpg)

Like raw meat, veggies, and fruits!
