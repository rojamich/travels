This was a much easier feat than we are used to because we are traveling with only one backpack each! We ALMOST made it to our hotel when two doors down we spotted a scrumptious Ramen spot and were seated right away. Just ahead of me becoming hangry :P

After getting settled, we headed over to reunite with a wonderful group of goofballs that we were lucky to meet originally in Savannah, Georgia! Many of us live all over the U.S. now but still participate in a fantasy football league. This was the second annual fantasy draft that _almost_ everyone was able to meet for! It was such a great night filled with laughter and sh*t talk. Almost all are/were military members so would you expect anything less??

It was such a cool experience and the ferry ride over only took 15 minutes! The first set of stairs (183) took us to the top of the pedestal (or you can take an elevator). The next set was a narrow spiral staircase(162 stairs) that wound our way up to the top of the crown and gave us an overview of the cityscape and bay.
