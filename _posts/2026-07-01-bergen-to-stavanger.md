Let's kick our last day in Bergen off with a run! I found a viewpoint that I could run to and decided that would have to happen. 😍 I stepped outside to start my run and it was sprinkling... rain run??

It was allllll uphill baby. While I knew it would be some uphill, I didn't think my whole legs would burn!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783274976/20260702_064228_uymzrc.jpg)

If you can believe it, many times I had a choice between stairs or a long ramp and chose the stairs! I still suck at stairs 

It was so green everywhere! How lovely it is here!

It was raining on my way back and really capped off the run for me!

We packed up and headed to the ferry that would take us to Stavanger, Norway for the next adventure! We went through metal detectors and two security guards searched our bags before we boarded. They were sweet and complimented my pack job but I can't tell if they were making fun of me or not! My laundry was loose in there from the mornings run 

We paid for a lounge pass on the ferry and had a quieter experience inside. There was a door separating us from the karaoke next door - what! It was a five hour ferry (ugh, I always feel terrible with the seasick patch - though, not as bad as I would without it!)

The wifi was impossibly slow and the cocoa was just ok. At least the cooca was pretty!

One ferry and two buses later, we were at Hertz rental for our rental car! Mike has almost driven on every continent! Probably won't hit Antarctica driving but who knows 😂

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783276084/IMG-20260702-WA0043_bk5kuz.jpg)

We found our way to the hotel room and showered/passed out hard...until the loudest alarm began going off at 1230am! FIRE ALARM are you kidding me? Eleven flights of stairs later and winding our way through the staff hallways to find the lobby... the entire hotel evacuated and they opened the train station lobby next door for us all to wait in because it was raining!

Oy. It was only an hour later that we were re-shuffled back into the hotel. The lines for the elevators were long but we finally made it back upstairs for some more deep sleep.
