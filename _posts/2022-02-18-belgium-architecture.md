We wandered around the city in search of Jeanneke-Pis. The statue was hidden in a well-lit alley in an odd-shaped outdoor shopping hall . It was said to have been erected in honor of gender equality due to their being a manneken-pis. Once this one was created, people began throwing coins in the fountain and a non-profit organization started collecting the money and donating it to different organizations. To learn more about this, you can read more at: visit.brussels/en/
