Up at 415am with excitement, we hit the park nearby for an early workout! Its been fun to get creative and mix things up between running, dancing, jungle gym equipment, hiking, and kayaking! Up way too early and ready to go, we headed to the train station early to find breakfast and snacks for the journey. 

Today we started the train ride from Oslo to Bergen! What a FREAKING DREAM !!! The first train will take us to Myrdal, where we will transfer to  a train to Flam. That’s where we will have a tour to the top of a fjord and stay the night!

I’m currently writing on the train, soaking up the breath-taking views, and stopping to read my book. Excuse me while I kiss Mike with utter gratitude and excitement!

The view started in the forest...

Suddenly, Hundra appeared! The seductive forest spirit from Norse Folklore! According to legends (which we learned about the next day) these spirits were said to lure travelers into the forest with her beauty and songs. It was such a cool and unexpected experience!

The train began to follow a river flowing through the cut in the mountains, how alluring!
