We woke up about 530am and I set off for a morning run to try and kick the grogginess. We had a 2.5 hour drive ahead of us to find Cooks Beach aka Hot Springs Beach! We were worried about the weather as it promised to rain for days!

  


We winded through the mountains and finally made it to the beautiful coast.
