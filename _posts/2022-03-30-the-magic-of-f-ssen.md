The next day was Füssen! We got the heck outta the hotel and headed for the train station for our train at 930am! We snacked on treats from Starbucks and some coffee before it was time for the two-hour train ride to the Neuschwanstein Castle! We wanted to get there early to hike around the area before our tour at 3pm. The castle was what Disney based theirs off of! It was actually built by mad King Ludwig II whose manner of death was never confirmed. The ride up there gave us some beautiful views and a sneak preview of how cold it was going to be up there! See the snow??

We caught the bus that ran once an hour, up to the drop off at the bottom of the mountain that leads to the castle. It was a steep little walk. We found out the bridge hike was closed so we couldn’t go exploring ☹

It was still really beautiful up there and I wish we could’ve gotten a better vantage point of it!

We hiked up the steep pathway and were told there were no early entries – Tours ran every 5 minutes! We got some photos at the bottom and waited patiently for our turn. The tour was fairly quick and we used these interesting devices that allowed the tour guide’s voice to project to each of us individually. It was helpful! We learned about the unfinished castle and how the King barely occupied it before passing away. The castle was then turned into a museum. 

The view from the balcony was grand!
