I armed my alarm for 5am to have enough time for the run hike to the waterfall today! It still isn't getting dark out so our eye covers have been total life savers! We've been tracking our sleep stats with our [aura rings](https://ouraring.com/?srsltid=AfmBOopTUU-ll55c0i8PnknwPKVcy9vpprdOukP1Ri3fq5zN3DNp5WNz) too and it's amazing how the cold influences if we sleep well or not. No AC means adapting and changing a lot of sleep conditions!

The town and roads were empty this morning and I savored the peace that comes with early mornings. I love the sense of having the world all mine for a moment!

My run took me past the fjord.

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782816312/20260629_051244_gkrmdc.jpg)

this little neighborhood.

and... what is that...?

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782815328/20260629_052116_qjfpce.jpg)

FREAKING HIGHLAND COWS???? ARE YOU KIDDING ME?? This is my dream come true! It's on my bucket list to see highland cows and here they are on a random running route in the middle of Norway?? Looks like a trip to Scotland just got pushed back a bit...

So I arrived at the foot of this hike...and what greets me? Freakin' stairs. 587 of them to be exact. 

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782816494%2F20260629_052848_myxins.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782816496%2F20260629_053127_wjxb7r.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

I'm dying. Surely, this is how it ends. My stomach lurches and threatens to upchuck here on this never-ending staircase. Why have I done this?

Mercifully, the stairs open up into this view of the fjord and this rushing waterfall!

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782816576%2F20260629_053951_hqcgva.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782816580%2F20260629_054028_lhw1wf.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

Hands-on-knees, gasping for air like an asthmatic, I gather myself for a selfie to mark the moment I voluntarily climbed stairs.

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782816656%2F20260629_054020_hylqs6.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782816667%2F20260629_054035_qdnflp.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

Tempted to run back down all 587 stairs, I realize a broken knee would be bad for the next two weeks of Norway and barely resisted. Heading back into town, there was a gentleman walking his excitable puppy and I got to pet a dog, finally. Again, the dogs here do not care about human affection or even other dogs. Astounding. No barking, no jumping, etc. Wild.

All ready for the day, we headed downstairs for our buffet breakfast - it was a massive variety of odd and normal breakfast options (including cake???!!)

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782816932/20260629_065748_kwpuow.jpg "The eggs even tasted good! ")

Early on in our trip here, I looked up cantaloupe season in Norway to see if I should get some...There is no cantaloupe season here lol. Of course, there is no cantaloupe season here  Its all imported. Talk about spoiled Californian!

We had a smidge of time before our morning adventure so I finished my newest paperback and put it in the hotel book selection for someone else to enjoy! Mike said it's been cool to see me do that because it's like leaving little pieces of me everywhere 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782817211/20260629_084345_mcz7ak.jpg)

Our fjord tour was coming up so I headed to get a spot in line while Mike checked us out of the hotel. I thought I was in for a long line ahead of me as it was 30 minutes 'til boarding but there were just people milling about... I wove my way through the rope-lines like a rollercoaster line and got first in line! WHAT! A truly lovely surprise! Immediately, I set something off and the fellow tourists piled in behind me. Before we knew it, the rope lines were completely full!

I dropped our luggage in the luggage rack because this was not just a tour but a transportation to our next town, too! Mike grabbed us a front row seat for best fjord viewing and door access. The ride was so smooth I felt zero water-rocking us! I sipped some average cocoa and tried this chip flavor 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782817491/20260629_094520_b7nae2.jpg "Its just salt XD - isn't that all chips??")

We wove through "Norway's grand canyon" and saw so many different waterfalls and waterways. The green of the water was exquisite!

The weather was really warm with the sun shining at first!

But then the cloud coverage came out and it went straight threw us! Either way, it was utterly beautiful and even more than I expected from the fjords here. We were blown away by the beauty!

This multi-layered waterfall was so cool to see! How it flows off the plateau of the mountains and all the way down to the valley!
