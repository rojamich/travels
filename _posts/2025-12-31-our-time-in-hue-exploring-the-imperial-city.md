Our guide was very knowledgable of the kings, their reign, and their demise. She walked us through each of them. Sometimes, I got confused momentarily because she pronounced Dynasty as DIE Nasty. I would say I can only imagine how my Vietnamese was interpreted by her, but I know exactly how my Vietnamese was interpreted lol. I wanted to say it was cold here, and we went back and forth saying the word Lanh - it sounded like I was saying it correctly, to me! Then we had a bit of role reversal where she pointed to the rain on the ground and asked if we had flirting in the US... I was unsure and asked, "Flirting?" We kept saying it back and forth until I asked, "Flooring?" Does she mean marble flooring?
