Our guide wanted to show us giant lily pads! After a boat ride, we marched into the woods of a swampy area where the mosquitoes absolutely swarmed. We were beating them back constantly. Even as I type this on the airplane, my arms feel like they should be flailing about. It was worth seeing these monstrous lilies!! An added bonus was the huge woodpecker on the walk back! I didn't know they were so big! So cool to see it actually make a hole!

Once back at the boat, our guide moved locations and we were shown how to fish for piranha... in the same water we had swam in earlier that day...

He explained they won't just bite anything that enters the water. Anything will bite if you stick your finger in its mouth LOL

The movie Piranha really did a number on those guys' reputations.

  


![](https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/a/AVvXsEgxG3HSMcec1Yw3pKg5gBx8C6wQRDAgS2ykeyCOi-NCsZVDvIfw15BmOIrZPxyuar3waaISBPAzlpQ-g5l0aOWjIFe5ISaXETN-k62NFP9FOlZTogCtHdyf5A0pk1EZs-NzJIJLBmotV8L3R1atHn5Dfo2-8LUXkbK0LCZZjC4niQilrkRvIvd_ihQcxLs=w300-h400)

The first cast off from our group was a success, and she hauled one in! Our guide showed us the razor-sharp teeth that protrude from the fish's gums. 

  


We jetted off to another fishing spot once the piranhas showed they're experts at removing bait and avoiding hooks here lol.

  


We had way better luck at our new spot, with Mike and me hauling in 5 fish each! More than just piranhas, too! It was so cool to feel them tug on the line viciously, thanks to the chicken bait. I do not like fishing whatsoever, but it was crazy knowing these little devils were in there.

Somehow, everyone had enough energy for a night hike where we marched deep into the jungle to see what came out at night. The couple we were with had a tendency to look for nature instead of where they stepped...this often led to them tripping, sinking into the mud, getting vines trapped on them, or stepping on every single loud and crunchy leaf! Thankfully, we still spotted a lot of wildlife! I was a bit worried they would fall or get hurt since many trees have painful spines!
