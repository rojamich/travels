This was the first time I had planned an event for Mike's family and I was so nervous about screwing up! Mario (Mike's dad) had the ultimate bucket list items to check off. 1) Visit Greece and 2) Scuba diving with his kids to see statues. So three got certified in preparation for the trip, and Mike and I were already certified. Three others committed to snorkeling while we scuba dove in the same area. So there was a lot riding on this!

We arranged for a taxi van to take all 8 of us to Trogir, Croatia - about 30 min away from where we docked in Split, Croatia.

I had talked to the company 4 months earlier, then 1 month ago, and then two days ago to make sure the reservation is complete. But this morning, they were not responding. Mike and I scoured the area looking for the company, talked to their sister company, called the company number, and finally got a text from the company stating the reservation was not confirmed. I was FREAKING OUT. No no no! Not cancelled!

We went back and forth overtext and I sent them the email confirmations and text confirmation and she finally came back saying "OH! You are Jenna!" Thank the lord. Our reservation was safe and the boat Captain was nearby so we loaded up onto the boat and headed out to the island nearby.

It was just us and a couple free divers that left quickly after we arrived.

Our gear was prepared and the snorklers were able to head right out! The scuba divers helped each other dress in gear and head out into the water to expore!

The museum was called stations of the cross and we brought a gopro to document Mario's bucketlist come true.
