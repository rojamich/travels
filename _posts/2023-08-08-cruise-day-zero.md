Arriving at the port with time to spare was very unexpected! We were prepared for flight delays, train delays, trouble returning the rental car, bus delays, etc but everything shockingly went smoothly!

We received directions from the information desk at the train station and had to stand half of the 85 minute train ride to the port and tried to adjust to the weather (we came from the 50s-70s and arrived in the 90s) Ouch!

  


Rather than take a free bus to the port (we had sat a lot that day) we walked 40 minutes to our ship. My motto is, if I can see it, I can walk to it (even if it's ridiculously inaccurate and has been a lesson learned many a time). It was further than expected and hotter than I was ready for but we made it much earlier than expected. When we looked around the ship we were perplexed, where is the line? There was a several hour long line to get into our last cruise but we waltzed through security and right onto the boat!
