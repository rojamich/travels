We finally found a burger place that looked promising and were on our way there when a sight stopped me in my tracks! Cosmic Comedy Club! The sign out front said English Comedy Night: 11.03 7pm-8pm. It was 705pm. What luck! Would we be let in even though its late? The doorman told us the comedy starts at 9pm (oh my god that’s late!) and two-for-one drinks were from 7-8. Mike looked at me like its ok, I know we really aren’t going to do this and I told the doorman, yes! We do want two tickets!  I thought Mike was going to fall over in surprise.

The show went on until just after 11pm and the dj came on to say everyone should stay and drink with them etc and I was like omg its so effing late! Get my ass home baby! We crashed pretty hard and slept in the next morning.

We woke up and headed to our first stop of the day, Checkpoint Charlie. There were a lot of people near a new exhibition a block over so we were grateful to see there was an actual museum to visit as well (next to the checkpoint).

From the American side, you can see the view of the Soviet side with a Soviet soldier.

 I was granted a student discount for my ticket! Thought that was pretty cool. The Museum was packed with information. It was one of my absolute favorite museums because it was just so interesting!

The most interesting part of the museum was that it was filled with the variety of ways that East Berliners escaped into West Germany. Some of those included hiding people in a speaker box, suitcases, a car seat, kayaking around the wall, a family zip-lined across and my personal favorite- hot air ballooned.
