The next morning, Michael navigated our way to the correct bus stop to take us to a cute café. The bus stop had three different bus lines waiting for people to be loaded. Michael was determining which bus was ours with an “AHA! That’s the one!” The doors began to shut at that very moment. With my public transportation experience being limited to DC metros and NY subways, I knew sometimes you had to jam your way in closing doors if you wanted to get on the metro! Unfortunately/fortunately, this is not the case for London buses as I discovered bewildered passengers staring at me and a disgruntled bus driver explaining all I had to do was knock on the door and she would’ve reopened it. Whoops lol.
