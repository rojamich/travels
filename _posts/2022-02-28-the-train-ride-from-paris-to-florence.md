The photos do not do it justice! Dang glare from the lights on the train! Can you see the tiny frozen waterfalls in the second photo?

I found our first portion of the train ride to be a bit cramped and Mike did not have enough leg room. At the stop at the station to transfer to our connection towards Florence, we found a café where we had an abysmal lunch. Neither of us were satisfied with this and grabbed a couple snacks from a vending machine on the next platform. This is where we tried a bar called “Bueno”! and bueno they were! They were similar to kitkats but I think it had caramel in it. Mike says kitkats taste like poop now, in comparison.

The second half of the train ride was in business class and omg. Ruined for all other train rides. So dang comfortable, we had a table between us to rest our electronics, plenty of space to charge and Mike had the ultimate leg room. I proudly finished writing the 26 pages (with photos) about our trip to Paris and unknowingly, loudly announced it to Mike. He asked me if I needed to pop my ears LOL! I had no idea I was broadcasting that to the entire cable car.

I haven’t addressed the dog situation here or in the UK yet. None of the dogs care about other people. They don’t get excited to see other people or want you to pet them. They don’t even care about other dogs. It’s astounding to me! I want to pet all the dogs, dammit! They also, for the most part, all wear these adorable little jackets and sweaters because its cold here for them!
