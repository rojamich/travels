We woke up the next morning under the agreement there would be nothing accomplished this day. Recovery for the win! We joked that this was a vacation from the vacation. We tour hard! They had a breakfast upstairs in the castle in the mornings that was a huge spread of fancy foods. Fruits, rolls, cereals, boiled eggs, yogurts, meats and cheeses! I was in heaven finally being able to eat a good breakfast finally! We had juice too and even tried Raspberry and orange jams!

Straight back to the room for us this day! The tub was huge and perfect for soaking. We enjoyed a day of reading our books. I couldn’t believe I finished the book I got in England! 

I started The Diary of Anne Frank this day with the hope I could finish it before we got to the Anne Frank house in Amsterdam. I wanted to understand what her family went through, on a deeper level and Mike learned vicariously through me.
