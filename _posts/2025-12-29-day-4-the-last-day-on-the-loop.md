This morning we woke up exhausted! I'd had a persistent cough that had kept us up half the night. Hopefully the rest of the hostel couldn't hear me :/

It was our last night on the road and we woke up determined to be present and enjoy our last day on the bikes! We had a bit of time before breakfast so we played chess. Though, for me, it was more of a refresher of what each piece does XD I'm not good at chess!

Breakfast this morning was a hot ramen with eggs - great for warming up and getting some protein in first thing :)

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1780938594/20251229_082211_tvqqey.jpg)

We loaded up, eager for what the day would bring! Our guides were perky and happy which made the morning fun too. Our first stop led us to an elevated viewpoint - this means A LOT of stairs! We walked about a half-mile of stairs and then about 5 flights of stairs to get to the foggy top!

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938685%2F20251229_110745_npobef.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938684%2F20251229_091316_crycg4.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938801%2F20251229_091136_bx5cnz.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938789%2F20251229_091744_vyohd7.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

One of my favorite views in Oregon is when the fog is settled between the fir trees. I just feel so at peace and in awe that we live somewhere so beautiful and green. This was a nice opportunity to stop and appreciate our presence here. Sometimes, on trips and in life, I'm just thinking about what's next. Planning, prepping, stressing. Times like these let me calm my mind and just be. 

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938850%2F20251229_093106_yi2g4h.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938755%2F20251229_093433_l8i3qv.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

Back on the road, we hit a bit of traffic ;) I was always nervous the wild life would decide they didn't appreciate us on their path! 

It was then that something unexpected happened!

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938746%2F20251229_094538_qqt5we.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938795%2F20251229_094355_mvnz7u.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

My? My Guides? The bike we were riding on broke down! Here is my guide working to fix it. Mike's guide loaded up ***all three of us*** on his bike and took us to the nearest coffee stop :o. This is also about the time where the fantasy football championship was coming to an end. I was neck and neck with another team for the championship game and I was one pass away from taking the lead in the last five minutes of the game. My stress level was at an all time high! My competition was also the biggest crap-talker of the league. Losing would burn me good. 

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938670%2F20251229_094932_sazznv.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938731%2F20251229_094950_slrsp1.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22Here%20I%20am%20pretending%20I'm%20not%20stressing%20out%20and%20just%20trying%20to%20roll%20with%20the%20day!%20Though%20my%20butt%20did%20hurt%20from%20sitting%20on%20the%20rack%20instead%20of%20a%20seat!sitting%20on%20the%20%22%7D %}

Thankfully the coffee shop wasn't too far away and it was pretty nice! We ordered some tea to warm up and admired the view (while constantly refreshing the fantasy football app). 

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780938722%2F20251229_100343_qj328l.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780940153%2F20251229_104227_ahisdb.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

I needed to use the restroom and discovered there was one you could pay for or there was a free one. They were *vastly* different XD. I would rather use a less-awesome bathroom if it means saving the $ for a future coffee haha. (Of course, there are limitations). I had to take a rickety wooden ladder down to this "restroom". And it kindof looked like somewhere Pennywise the clown might inhabit. 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1780938830/20251229_103559_pv3le0.jpg "If you haven't seen or read \"IT\" by Stephen King, you are missing out... on a brilliant story but also nightmares.")

To my surprise, I was not devoured by a fear-munching clown and scurried back upstairs to safety. To my utter heartbreak, I lost the fantasy championship by .04 of a point. One completed pass would have won me the season. While I am/was devastated, second for the second year in a row ain't bad. Here's to next season!!

Back on the road, we wound through the foggy and chilly countryside while admiring the jagged rock formations!

We had a quick lunch that was warm and scrumptious! Everyone in this village seemed to smoke - I forgot how common this is in other countries!

Our last stop before bussing back to Hanoi led us down some crazy roads! Mike and I looked at each other like... will we make it??

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780941991%2F20251229_133948_x7gk3v.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780942003%2F20251229_133951_zsmvuq.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

After a very bumpy and slippery off-roading trip, we made it to a beautiful field where we were led onto our hiking trail by Mike's guide. It ended at this really cool waterfall!

I really liked my guide for the trip. Mike's seemed more veteran at the job and put forth less effort. But Mike really liked him and they vibed well together, thankfully! Our views on the way back to the bikes were SO cute! Look at these cows grazing and this little curious guy! I wanted to stop and hang out/pet them for the rest of the day haha!

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780941254%2F20251229_141832_mljqgg.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780941291%2F20251229_142124_um7428.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

We were finally on our way to the final hostel - not to stay but to get scooped up by the bus to Hanoi. We were able to see a few neat sights on the way too!

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780941219%2F20251229_142826_mqja05.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780941205%2F20251229_142848_wncikk.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1780941212%2F20251229_143134_lm1w5i.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22Creating%20a%20roof%20by%20collecting%20tree%20branches%2Fleaves%20and%20a%20massive%20pro-communism%20sign!%22%7D %}

It was time to say our goodbyes to our guides and, after several hugs, we watched them ride away. What an amazing experience - a real once-in-a-lifetime adventure that we won't forget! I'm so glad we did this! We waited for the bus and played backgammon/ate Oreos until it arrived :) Yes, they were for sale at this hostel! Neapolitan flavor?? Yes, please!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1780943630/20260104_211745_hwbmi4.jpg)

It was roughly 4 hours back to our hotel and we were so happy to see this giant bed :o Why is it covered in a heart with rose petals? Because we were celebrating (late) our 13 years of marriage! Wahoo!
