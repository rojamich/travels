We decided to board the train from the Brussels-Nord station this time to save our lives (hey you would assume the other station would be even worse early in the darkness!) 

We were fairly lost when we arrived at the Bruges train station but thankfully found an incredibly helpful employee who broke down the tickets, told us where we needed to depart and return from and explained everything to us! She even helped us with our French pronunciation haha.  A one-hour train ride later, we were in the most beautiful place I have ever seen! I love Bruges!

To answer your question, yes, it was colder there lol. London was high 40s high 50s but it was low 30s here in the morning and low 40s in the afternoon. The Minnewater was even prettier in person and Mike says he will remember this morning as so cold that the fog was sitting on top the water. That completes the picture of that morning because it was a little ominous with the church bells chiming as we walked alone in the cold. And I emphasize alone because it felt like we were the only two in the whole city that morning. We really enjoyed having the beauty and peace of the city to ourselves for a while. 

Breakfast was really cool and unique experience for us! I believe I found this restaurant on reddit and we took a chance on it. Thankfully it had just opened because we were hungry! The restaurant was called Carpe Diem, and boy did we!
