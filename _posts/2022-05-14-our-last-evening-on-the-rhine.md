Some things we wish we had done/known for Sankt Goar. 

We could have done a day or two more here if more restaurants were open and if it were summer, I would’ve wanted to get out on the water! The time we spent here was just a few days but we made it feel like a week! Mike says it feels like we spent more time in Sankt Goar than we did in Rome. The smell of the candles burning, rose petals that surrounded us, the hot water of the bath and good books to read with delicious chocolate. It was all so perfect.

Cars were few and far between here but it was cool that they have a vehicle ferry to take the cars back and forth across the river!

You had to be fully vaccinated to travel here, have a negative COVID test or proof of recovery. They began lowering the mask mandates in some areas of the hotel while we were here. 

Both Labara and EE sim cards worked here. 

The water in the restaurants costs to drink, it does not come with your meal for free. No public restrooms that we saw. 

Everyone we talked to spoke German with a little English.

Days visited: March 5th-8th

Daily weather: 20s in the morning and high 30s during the day.

Transportations used: walking! 

Total distance walked: 13.96 miles
