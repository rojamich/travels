What a day of relaxation! We took today off, there was supposed to be a big rainstorm! So it felt fitting to call it a down day. 10 hours of sleep later, a ton of reading (steam deck playing for Mike) and a walk for gelato – there was never a rainstorm lol!

That night we decided to try Schroder’s restaurant – the favorite haunt of Harry Hole from the Jo Nesbo serial killer series. We read all the novels and they just released the first season on Netflix!! 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782065171/20260620_204134_piw2ie.jpg)

We were met with an enthusiastic and hilarious maître d. He was kind and patient, teaching us Norwegian and explaining where each part of the restaurant was used in the series. Thankfully, there was a menu in English. They brought over “bread” and butter for the table – it was unleavened bread! I thought it tasted like bread with a chip texture. Mike said it tasted and feltlike chips. The butter was SUPER salty but delicious. 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782065167/20260620_192939_g8b3si.jpg)

Mike asked if they served Jim Beam there (Harry’s signature drink) and he looked at him blankly and said of course (add dramatics!). To fully immerse, he got a Jim Beam and a beer chaser – just like Harry XD

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782065081/20260620_192016_wo0auv.jpg)

The entrance had photos of the filming set, Harry’s favorite table had a label, and even the bathroom had a newspaper clipping in it!

I played it safe by ordering the steak, the maître d looked at me expectantly and asked medium rare? To Mike’s shame, I asked for medium well. Mike got the pork dish! 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782067789/20260620_191419_njdv8l.jpg)

Apparently, this is medium well.. Mike, thankfully, switched with me because his bacon looked fantastic and my steak was made to his liking. How did I get so lucky??
