When we became hungry again, we found a little Italian restaurant where the waiter was a crack up and the food looked awesome. I tried something new for the first time! Gnocchi is what its called and we learned later why I love it so much… its mostly potato! I could probably successfully live in Ireland and my dad would probably say I could successfully live in Idaho lol. This was one of my favorite dishes in Florence!

Here is Mike trying a pasta dish in Italy for the first time! Unfortunately, he did not enjoy it. We learned that most dishes in Northern Italy do not have as much sauce on them (In comparison to France/America). The ingredients speak for the dish itself. I thought his “sauce” was very good. It was a beef and mushroom pasta.
