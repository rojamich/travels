The view from there at night is truly gorgeous. It’s the perfect spot to see the Eiffel Tower and Paris lit up at night (just after our hotel room!)

After an incredibly romantic evening, we headed to a highly recommended reddit pizza place called Inizio. Thankfully, we had called ahead for reservations! This is where I tried the most diverse pizza I have ever tasted! And I loved it! Minus the walnuts lol. It was bacon, ham, pears, honey and walnuts! Definitely must try at home!
