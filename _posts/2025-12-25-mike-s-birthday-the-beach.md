They were cute little blue cruisers, and our first stop was the Bahn Mi Queen - it is our last day in Hoi An, so we were soaking up all the best spots! It was a surprisingly nice and relaxing bike ride out to the beach! With how hectic the inner city was, this was a good change of pace.
