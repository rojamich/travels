Breakfast had an array of choices along with a chef who made eggs by request - yay more eggs (seriously, I eat them every day at home!)

*5 Weeks Earlier...*

Before heading off to Vietnam, I got the itch to get another tattoo! After some research and brainstorming together, we came up with the idea to do a tattoo based on all my favorite books! I was SO excited! After finding a good shop, we sent off my concept and conversed with the artist via whatsapp.

We found the [Silver Ant Tattoo Shop](https://www.instagram.com/silveranttattoo/) and they were so kind and professional! They were very detail-oriented and got back to me quickly with a design concept.

*Back to Present Day...*

It's the morning of my tattoo appointment and it's only a ten minute walk from our hotel! Amazing that Mike plans things out so well!

The walk over was peaceful and quiet. The roadways are filled with pedestrians and tuk tuks later so this was a nice surprise! It was also much cooler here in Hanoi - the 60s compared to HCMC where it was in the 80s! The perfct temp as far as we are concerned :)

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1780946380/20251230_083932_ezk6cl.jpg)

 We made it to the shop and met with the manager to discuss last minute details of the tattoo. One that stuck out in my mind is the detail of the snowman. It was a really cheerful looking guy and I had to explain this was from a serial killer series ([Jo Nesbo](https://jonesbo.com/)) so, perhaps he could look less joyful? The details were just perfect then and [Rita](https://www.instagram.com/rita.silverant/) began setting up the chair for work. 

I settled in after they said it would likely be an all-day job! Here is the outline before the ink :)
