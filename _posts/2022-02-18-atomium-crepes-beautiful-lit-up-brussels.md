We had a relaxing train ride back to Brussels from Bruges and once we arrived at the station, we rushed over to Atomium which Michael was overly excited for since we decided to visit Belgium! It was even more massive than I could have imagined! The day was beautiful out. 

Finding the ticket box was unexpectedly complicated! Finally in line for the display inside Atomium, we were underwhelmed by some boring history of the designers and a lights display. Not really sure how this was a “must see” for visiting here. The view was vast but not sure it was worth the effort.

Also, I fell down an entire flight of stairs here and scared the heck out of two, very kind, Parisian girls.

On the car ride back to our Airbnb we heard a woman yelling and saw two teens running off with her shopping bags. She was chasing them unsuccessfully and we heard sirens begin. We thought it would be cool if they were responding to that! Turns out, there was a “Polite” van right there and they began pursuing them! Go Belgian police! 

We made our way back to the city center and found a very fancy restaurant that served mussels and crepes, two of the necessary items to check off our list! 

Michael and I both tried (yes, BOTH!) mussels. Apparently, mussels and frites are a major meal here in Belgium. I found the texture weird and disliked them. Mike said they’re fine lol.
