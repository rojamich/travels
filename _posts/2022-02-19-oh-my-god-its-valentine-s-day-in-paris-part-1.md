It was hard to find French restaurants in town using google so we had to walk and find them on our own, which was fun and adventurous! The reviews from others seem to mean nothing to us as 3.8 stars tasted like 4.5 to me! That morning we had a restaurant that became my favorite spot there! Café de Tullaries 💓 Mike had the best Nutella crepe known to mankind and I ordered a delicious Croque Madame. This is only different from a Croque Monsieur in that it has an egg on top!

Once we got closer to the Tower, it was an entirely different story. Scammers, pick pockets and throngs of tourists were pushing their way around us to get to the line to entire the Tower. Neither of us had a desire to go into the tower because the view was really _the_ Tower. We already had tickets to see the city from Paris’s only skyscraper – the Montparnasse Tower, later that week.

We did, however, successfully upset several would-be scammers around the Tower. A girl in her 20s was charging through the crowd- not moving for anyone and forcing others to move around her. I couldn’t move anywhere and I shoulder checked her as she tried to go through me. She was irate that anyone would dare not move around her! Another gentleman was aggressively trying to sell things and I firmly said no! He cursed me out and was upset because I didn’t even know what he was selling!
