After which we bussed to our second boat for the actual cruise! During the bus ride over, the driver announced that conservation was big here in NZ and to please do your part while driving and any opossum, wild cats, stoats etc you see, please use your car to turn them into a squisher! Apparently they are destroying the bird life here.
