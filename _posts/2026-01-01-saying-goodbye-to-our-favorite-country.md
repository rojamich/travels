Today was the day we would say goodbye to our favorite country. I was absolutely not ready to do so. I fell head over heels for this country, their culture, their language, and *especially* the food!

We had time for me to exercise this morning so I headed to the huge park nearby for some dance!

The aunties were out in full force this morning, doing their own dance routine. I hope we live here someday so I can join them 🩰💃👯‍♀️

I saw someone record me dancing and was approached by a girl who asked which line dance I was doing?! I told her fuego and she said she loves that one! She dances in San Fran! What a coincidence on the timing too!!!

We stopped by the same food spot as the day before and ate some more Bánh Cuốn! It was delightful and I was sad I only discovered it at the end of our trip. Here's to more Bánh Cuốn in our future!

I was looking to get a mani pedi this morning to cap the trip off right but no where had openings 😥 At least we saw some interesting street market sales going on!

The sidewalks might be for walking, selling, eating, or ... even parking!

Everything was delicious and felt like saying goodbye. We cherished every bite and every sip. We also tried to get one last massage but that didn't work out either!

As we were preparing to leave, I was able to capture a couple photos I'd been trying for the whole trip! One was the way they carry fruit and the other was how a family of four could ride one bike together!

We made it to the airport and it was beautiful!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783003015/20260102_153243_tcq4qy.jpg)

We headed towards the lounge and this was the line to get in 🤯

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783363177/20260102_174037_y5vxuu.jpg)

We managed to get in and find a good table in a quiet back room area. The food offered was plentiful and good!

The lounge had everything you might need, including a shower! I must feel clean before flying.

We also saw my friend from San Fran that I'd met in the park that morning! The four of us exchanged names and talked about our travels!

It was an easy 3.5 hour flight to Singapore for a few days stopover. Feel free to join on the next blog to read about it!
