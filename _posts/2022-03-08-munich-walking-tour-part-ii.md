We moved on to the Asam Church which is a small model church! You could come here and pick out things to decorate your church with! The audio tour even pointed out which items were real and which were fake (made to look fancy). I really liked how they explained this on the tour without blatantly explaining it. They pretended like they were purchasers for churches and talked through the costs of items and their validity. It was so odd to see this church wedged between normal buildings, too!
