Some things we wish we had done/known for Paris, France. 

I would have liked one extra day for Paris to allow for rest after the 10-14 mile walking days. We walked no less than 10 miles every day in Paris.

The driving in Paris seemed relatively normal. No craziness except for the streets that didn’t have pedestrian walk lights. The cars didn’t yield for you if they didn’t have to. Most people expected you to get out of their way when walking. Regardless of what side of the sidewalk you’re on. 

The COVID restrictions were fairly relaxed as you didn’t need a negative test to come into the country. We arrived by bus and didn’t even have to show a passport. Masks were required most places other than smaller restaurants. The Westin was incredible as we sent them our information ahead of time and they were able to process our Californian COVID pass and get us European COVID passes. These have a QR code that is accepted in the UK and EU. We literally had to show them EVERYWHERE. Dining in, having a coffee sitting down, museums, hotels, towers. Everything! If you’re traveling soon to the EU, make sure to get one asap.

The Lebara and EE SIM cards both worked here. If you plan on doing any international calling, I would use the Lebara pass or get EE International calling plan. It was really useful so far having two different SIM cards here. When his didn’t work, mine did, and vice versa.

City Mapper wins again here and google maps was relatively useless. Also, don’t rely on google for restaurant searches. We noticed about half the restaurants aren’t on there and almost missed some good ones!

I preferred to walk everywhere I could in Paris but the Metro was easy to use (Though it was outdated/jerky and always packed to the brim)!

The water in the restaurants were hit and miss for cost. Some offered you tap water which was free. The fancier ones won’t offer you a choice. You can have still water or sparkling. The bathrooms are freely accessible though, not for charge. 

Days visited: February 13th\- 18th

Daily weather: High 40s in the morning and evenings, mid 50s in the middle of the day. It was really warm some days!
