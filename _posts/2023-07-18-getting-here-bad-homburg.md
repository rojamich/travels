We returned to Oregon for 20 hours before taking a flix bus to PDX for a redeye to NY. The flight there was comfortable on Jetblue and easy until...Storms in NY diverted us to Ohio. It was a rollercoaster of a flight plan between the unsurity of actually getting to NY, being deplaned and then halfway boarded before delayed again. Eventually we made it to NY 9 hours later than expected. Thankfully, we had planned to be there 36 hours before our next flight to Germany but we did miss our extra time in NY. As a bonus, we got to watch a passenger get arrested at the bar of the airport in Ohio while being blackout drunk at 10am. 

Thankfully, we were able to reschedule much of what we planned in NY for the remaining time we had there! We got in and immdiately napped as we were both experiencing migraines and exhausted. We woke up and went right to our escape room!
