Some things we wish we had done/known for Munich.

We had a fairly relaxed trip to Munich because we didn’t try to over schedule ourselves like we had for previous cities. 

The driving, walking, and biking are all highly regulated here with no surprises. We saw one person jaywalk and she was met by some angry honking! Don’t do it here! And don’t accidentally walk on the bike path. You _will_ be run over. 

The COVID restrictions were strict here. You had to be fully vaccinated, letter/proof of recovery within 90 days or a negative COVID test to enter any establishment. This was the first location that checked our Healthpass against our passports to confirm identity, as well.

The labara and EE sim cards both worked here. Citymapper was a godsend with the metros and bus routes/timing.

The metro here is perfect minus the complexity of the pass options. They also have multiple train/metro options! DB, ICE, Sbahn, and Ubahn! I wish the buses were on par with London, though. 

The water in the restaurants costs to drink, it does not come with your meal for free. The bathrooms are also for-pay use only.  

Most people here spoke a little English, which helped us to get by with Mike’s German!
