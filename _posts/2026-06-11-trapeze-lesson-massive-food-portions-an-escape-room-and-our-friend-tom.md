I hit the park bright and early for a run - who can resist a run through Central Park?! Contrary to Law & Order SVU, I did not get murdered. I did see a person on the ground, unmoving, being helped by EMTS who had raced through the park and up running trails to get to her!

I got to try out my new headphones which are so light-weight and have great sound quality - I'm super happy!

When I returned to our hotel, we tried a local bagel breakfast sandwich place - YUM! We each got an everything bagel with egg, ham, cheese (American and chive cream cheeses), and sausage. We definitely should have just gotten one. This thing was massive. 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1781432952/20260612_081303_1_pe54g8.jpg "The sheer weight of this thing was like being handed an infant unexpectedly.")

We ate as much as we could before heading to my trapeze lesson! You read that right, I'm prepping for the circus! Just kidding, we saw this online and Mike could tell I wanted to try it! AH! Looks so fun!

One subway ride later and three stories up, we were baking in the heat of NYC but very stoked.

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1781432955/20260612_104147_vgegqf.jpg)

We all harnessed up - there was a class of 5 but only one other person was brand new to this! Ross instructed us on the basics and told us to climb up the ladder to Ana at the top. The ladder climb was the most intimidating part lol

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1781432952%2F20260612_105737_lsgxyj.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1781432955%2F20260612_105735_b3uljh.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

The first trick we learned was swinging out, slipping our legs through our open arms, and hanging from our knee pits. I'm gunna be real, this is a lot harder than it looks. Do I routinely do hanging sit-ups? Not at all. Do I usually swing my whole body weight from my arms? No. Do I regularly slip my legs through my arms like an agile cat? No, no I do not. BUT, it was super fun! And challenging! And toasty!

{% youtube_block %7B%22url%22%3A%22https%3A%2F%2Fyoutu.be%2F7KFHd_Mw_lk%22%7D %}

Onto the next trick! We learned how to do a backflip off the bar! We did the entire first trick, then, after swinging back and forth, you use the momentum to flip backwards!

{% youtube_block %7B%22url%22%3A%22https%3A%2F%2Fyoutu.be%2F-4iS7XcC5zg%22%7D %}

Feeling maxed out, I was ready to call it a day! They said, wait! We have one more trick. Holy mama, they were not kidding. I couldn't believe we got to try this on our first lesson!

{% youtube_block %7B%22url%22%3A%22https%3A%2F%2Fyoutu.be%2FiGnJzjvfslI%22%7D %}

Everything was suddenly clear. When we met the crew, I was blown away by how buff these girls were and man! This is so physical! I'm so glad I tried this :) Mike was so supportive and shooting video the whole time and making sure I stayed hydrated.

We grabbed a sandwich on the way back and thank god we decided to split it!

We hustled back to our hotel to shower - I was drenched! And then back out again for our escape room - we seriously love doing these together. We think and approach things so differently its amazing to see and works so well to solve puzzles! PLUS, this was our third time at this escape room! We have been going here since we lived in Georgia!
