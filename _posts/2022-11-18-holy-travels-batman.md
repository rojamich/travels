Whew! A busy and tiring "3 days" of travel! Check-in and security were a breeze at our tiny Eugene airport and we had a 4 hour flight to Dallas. Flying inland just to fly west again makes less sense but I guess that's American Airlines International Hub. 

We got our seats upgraded so more leg room- yay! I knitted the four hours away. 

We made it to Dallas and were fascinated to see how different the international terminals were vs domestic flights. Way fancier- I had no idea there was a difference! We were grateful to use the priority pass for the lounge for snacks and quiet rooms to stretch before our long flight.
