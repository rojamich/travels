We had terrible customer service once we reached the Conciergerie! The guy at the desk checking tickets and COVID passes couldn’t even look up from his phone to help us and kept repeating himself and getting mixed up because he was so distracted! The other staff were pretty rude _and_ they closed the museum 10 minutes early. 

The Conciergerie was really interesting despite all of this. It was a fortress and French Revolution Prison and they even held Marie Antoinette prisoner there! This was the only time it rained on us the whole trip!
