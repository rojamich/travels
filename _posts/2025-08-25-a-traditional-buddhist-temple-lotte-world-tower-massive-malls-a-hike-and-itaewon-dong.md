It was time for a hike in Inwangsan Park! We took a bus close to the hike and walked a few blocks to get to the trailhead. There were no markers leading us, so Mike had to guess using the limited information available!

This hike contains the Seoul City Wall, Historic Site No. 10. It was originally built in 1396 to defend the capital during the Joseon Dynasty! To learn more about this fascinating time period, [read here](https://www.koreatodo.com/inwangsan-mountain)!
