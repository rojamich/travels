We started the day with another workout in the park and made friends! A little pug named Salt decided he wanted to join me along with his brother Pepper (a beagle mix) who demanded I throw the ball for him to fetch. WHAT A WIN!!

After making breakfast we caught a train into Frankfurt and transferred to one that brought us into Mainz. An easy ride and free wifi, wahoo! When we arrived, it was a short walk into town and we enjoyed seeing their different walk/stop signs!
