It was a beautiful day when we first arrived in the Netherlands and it was supposed to be raining, another win for us! The first thing Mike said when observing the city on the way to our BnB was that it appeared to be a Northern Venice because of all the canals. Later on, our walking tour Rick Steve would introduce the city as Amsterdam, the Venice of the North lol! The city was filled with colorful décor and an interesting variety of people. Overall, the Dutch are very tall! Here are a couple of cool sights we saw on our walk.

When we reached our Bed and Breakfast, our host was very excited and enthusiastic about our stay with well wishes for our anniversary and filled with recommendations. He even had two free bikes for us to use while we were there and breakfast vouchers for a restaurant down the street! What a host! I thought this was a cute personal touch. The room was tidy and filled with free tea too!

Our Bnb host recommended we try the Indonesian food since they were once one of the colonies here and you could get authentic Indonesian food! We decided to be adventurous and gave it a go for the experience! We each got a sample spread and went to town on trying the many meats, seasonings and spices. It was really cool to share that experience together!
