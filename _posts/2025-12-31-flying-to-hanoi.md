It was our last day in Hue, and our flight was delayed. We decided to take advantage of the time in Hue and started by enjoying the nearby park. Mike grabbed a coffee and brought his book while I danced!

There was a delicious looking streetfood stand by our hotel, so we took a chance and tried it!

There were only locals there, and they kept peering over at us, wondering what we were doing there. The same goes for our coffee spot in Hue.

There was a massage spot a few doors down from the hotel that we wanted to try out. There was a very young girl there to greet us, and she began rushing around to set everything up. We hoped she had someone to help her soon! Thankfully, someone else appeared shortly, and we were both able to get massaged and have my hairwashed! I cannot get over how good that feels and how nice it is not to have to do it myself. I am straight spoiled! We caught a Grab to the airport that looks like a pirate ship to us!

Security was a breeze here! We got through in under 5 minutes! Mike found the lounge we had access to, so we devoured some snacks and free water in the comfy quiet of the lounge. The flight was so short! 55 minutes! 

I'm not sure how I pictured Hanoi, but it wasn't like this!

Oh my goodness, the staff at our hotel were the sweetest and most bashful! They were so excited to greet us and startled when we used our few phrases of Vietnamese with them! They also served us fruit, mango, yum!!
