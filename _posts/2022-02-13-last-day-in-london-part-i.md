Our last day in London 😧! We walked to our Duck & Waffle breakfast reservation on top of one of London’s tallest buildings for a morning view and to watch the sunrise (well that was the plan, another overcast day but it was still lovely!)

The place was packed! Michael got a classic English breakfast and I tried the Waffles – Elvis Style.

We trotted over to the Tower of London which was vaster than I had pictured and was overwhelming in a good way. The staff there were comical and we walked around some before taking a free tour of the grounds (45 minutes raced by and felt like 5!) Our tour guide, the Raven Master, was brilliantly funny and gave us SUCH a good tour!
