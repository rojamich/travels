 We desperately needed water after that! We found out that the fountains around the city provided fresh, drinkable water from a two-thousand-year-old aqueduct! The water was so cold and refreshing. Here’s us excitedly trying it! We laughed saying it must be amusing for Romans to watch people excitedly try a water fountain. Imagine if someone took a selfie and admired the novelty of one of our water fountains lol.
