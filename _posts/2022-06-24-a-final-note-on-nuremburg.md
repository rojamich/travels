Some things we wish we had done/known for Nuremburg.

I could’ve done one more day here touring the city and making sure we didn’t miss any sites. I would’ve liked to find some better food places and rush around less. Only having one full day kindof limited us a bit but I still enjoyed what we had.

Masks were still required everywhere here. My skin was rough through our whole time in Germany due to the cold temps and my wearing jeans. If you go during this time of year make sure you to have lotion!

Michael’s Labara worked well in Germany and they offered another option called Vodaphone here. My EE worked most times but would not be my first choice if we lived in Germany. 

We mostly used google maps here because we only walked. The city was small enough for it.

The water in the restaurants was a mixture of tap (free) or bottle to purchase. It depended on the restaurant. The attractions we visited had free restrooms thankfully.

Days visited: March 8th-10th

Daily weather: Low 40s in the morning and evenings, mid 50s in the middle of the day.

Total distance walked: 16.56 miles

Transportations used: walking.
