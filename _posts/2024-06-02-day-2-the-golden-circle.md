Up early to beat the tour buses. The Golden Circle is the most famous day-trip from
Reykjavik, hitting three sights in a roughly 250 km loop.

## Thingvellir National Park

The site of the world's first parliament (930 AD!) and also the place where the North
American and Eurasian tectonic plates pull apart. You can literally walk between two
continents.

## Geysir

The original geyser — every other one in the world is named after this guy. The big one,
**Strokkur**, erupts every 5–10 minutes. We stood there for half an hour just watching it.

## Gullfoss

A two-tier waterfall that just keeps going. Photos don't do the scale justice.
