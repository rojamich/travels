Up next on the list was to visit Dusseldorf's "little Tokyo". For those who don't know, we are anxiously waiting to visit Japan next year and this was our chance to get a little of that in our system ;)

Mike looked online for a highly rated Japanese spot for lunch and found one called "Takumi". The decor inside was really cool and we were amped up for some ramen!

It was REALLY delicious and we were surprised there was no line! It was about 2pm so I reasoned it must be because it was an off time for lunch? Still, it was odd because the post referring to the spot said it was hard to get a table at all times!
