Holy moly what a day this was! We had a total of 9 hours off the ship and used up 8 of them! 

It was so hot, busy, and complicated but utterly beautiful. The water was so clear and the island of Mykonos is exactly what we picture when we think of Greece. The night before, they had a parade through the dinning hall. We all shouted for our rep, Candice, when she was introduced. She was the best!!

We got off the boat at about 9am and took the tender to the first port on Mykonos, we switched to two different ferries just to get over to Delos. This part had us missing Germany because it was literally 30 degrees hotter and they don't believe in lines or rules. We all just moved as a huge cluster everywhere. You had to fight, push, and shove to get on each ferry. People were using children as a shield to get ahead in lines! It was wild@!
