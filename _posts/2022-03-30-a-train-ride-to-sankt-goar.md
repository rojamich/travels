It was about 6 hours of train travel to get from München to Sankt Goar with several transfers and, due to delays, one missed train! At least the views were beautiful with the sunrise and then rolling along the Rhine Valley.

This resulted in us waiting at the station for about an hour for the next train to come by. Thankfully, the delay allowed us to take a similar train to the same location. We would be getting in a bit later but our rooms wouldn’t be ready until 6pm. 

We arrived at about one pm to a tiny station that was only two platforms! The “station” was one room that led to some stairs! The town was so cute and lovely!

We had to have lunch there since we certainly weren’t going back down that hill any time soon! It was way too fancy for me but Mike found a dish he liked. Our view was pretty great though! 

Our room was ready in the nick of time! The suite was the top room in the castle and the balcony was beautiful and intimate! I loved this room and the décor of flower petals, candles, and hearts!

The view from the balcony really takes your breath away. Good call on Mike to upgrade to the suite! It also came with a HUGE tub and free bath salts. It was all so relaxing. We also had a couch and chair to sprawl out in to read endlessly. The desk was helpful in the mornings to study our Duolingo languages! We were really cozy!
