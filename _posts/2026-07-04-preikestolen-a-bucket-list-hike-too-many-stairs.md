Feeling a whole bunch better, we packed up for our early hike! I was super hungry this morning too! 

This was hanging in our room and was the inspiration for a later pose on top of Preikestolen!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783343864/20260704_220316_czte2b.jpg)

They had a buffet breakfast with every jam or jelly or spread for the waffles but, somehow, no sauce for eggs 😮

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783344086/20260705_064554_dmnxqu.jpg "We tried their famous \"brown cheese\" here and it's actually really good! Its apparently, \"a sweet, caramel-tasted cheese-like food made by boiling whey, milk, and cream\"! Just what I love a combination of, sugar and cheese!")

The staff even filled our water bottles for us! It was drizzling on and off this morning so we were nervous about the view from the top.

The name of this hike is stairs, stairs, and more stairs. Ok, we should also add stone steps into the title as well.

We started early enough that there were very few people on the trail, hooray! If there were other people, they were either significantly slower or significantly faster than us. So the way up was really peaceful despite my ongoing feud with stairs. 

We alternated on who would carry the backpack to preserve both our energy and help us get in hiking shape for future challenging hikes. 

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1783344496%2F20260705_081350_u85s1t.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1783344497%2F20260705_083327_he1f5x.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1783344500%2F20260705_075418_lfcvhq.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

As we reached the last stretch of the hike, it got a bit narrow, windy, and scary!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783345236/20260705_083459_ctfvzn.jpg)

Mike, once again, got close to the edge and I had to ask myself if he's really been afraid of heights all these years or was faking??

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783345328/20260705_083939_tddxgs.jpg)

The pulpit was magnificent and even bigger/higher than I thought it would be! Truly a staggering sight to be hold! There weren't too many people and everyone was respectful of others' photos. Those who were clueless were quickly put in check!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783345414/20260705_084423_gng0mr.jpg)

Mike headed to get in line for a photo while I searched for someone to take it for us. No one stood out so I ended up taking his photos first. I think they turned out pretty cool!

He grabbed a couple shots of the world-altering view !
