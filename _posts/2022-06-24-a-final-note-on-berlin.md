Some things we wish we had done/known for Berlin.

We could have spent two more weeks in Berlin and not seen everything. Due to the transportation efficiency, amazing food, and lots of things to do, this is the most livable location for us.

The COVID restrictions were strict: you had to be fully vaccinated, show proof of recovery or have a negative test for every location/entry. 

Mike’s Labara SIM card worked more efficiently here than my EE Card.

City Mapper was fantastic here because there was so much transportation to choose from!

The trolleys were awesome here and took us straight from downtown to our Airbnb. If we had to go further, it was an easy connection through the metro.  

The water in the restaurants was a mix of free and for purchase. It depended on where you went. 

Days visited: March 10th – 14th

Daily weather: High 30s to mid 50s.

Total distance walked: 31.04 miles

Transportations used: metro, trolley, train, and walking. 

Trip total walking distance: 301.03 miles
