We had an appointment to go up inside the Berliner! The world’s largest TV antenna which was created to celebrate Soviet Rule. It’s huge and great views at the stop! Getting in was really simple with the tickets and no bags! You can even see Museum Island and my photo kinda makes it look like a poster!

On the metro, there was a dog I really wanted to pet. It looked up at me, then its owner. The owner nodded to it and it waddled over to me for pets!

We had a spa appointment this evening, our last night in Berlin. The location had massages, steam rooms, pools, jacuzzies, saunas, waterbeds and all kinds of lounge areas. Overall, it was a really comfortable spa and relaxing with a quiet atmosphere. I wish we had something similar to enjoy near us.

 This was the first time Mike said he just didn’t want to leave. We could’ve done two more weeks in Berlin and still not seen everything. I would go back in an instant and it has become one of my favorite cities.

 We didn’t see any homeless at all here in Berlin until we walked through an alley in a residential area towards the spa. There was a small encampment which was random but I wonder why they’re huddled there specifically.

     We are Amsterdam Bound!
