After we ground the beans into a paste, we were able to try tea with the shells, and three different types of hot cocoa with the paste - how fun!! We taste tested from this container of different chocolate types to see the stark differences of amounts in cocoa. 

  


100% chocolate nibs were insane! Apparently the percent from Cusco versus the Amazon were totally different! And depending on what the beans are grown next to, it influences the taste. So the ones grown in Cusco are grown next to coffee beans and are more bitter. The ones in the Amazon are grown next to fruit trees and is much sweeter. Mike prefers the white chocolate and the milk chocolate. I am more of a dark chocolate gal - but not 100% That's so bitter!
