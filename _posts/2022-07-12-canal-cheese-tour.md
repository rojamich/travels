They served us an aged gouda and a fresh gouda with spicy mustard and unlimited drinks. We floated down the river and learned about the history of Amsterdam while we ate our delicious cheese! We learned that 12 people drown in the canals every year (you’ll understand once you see the public restrooms), tons of biked fall in the canals every year due to weather and lack of securing, and there are more bikes than citizens in Amsterdam! 800,000 residents to 1.2 million bikes! The captain and the host were great and we had a really good time on here! So glad we signed up for this.
