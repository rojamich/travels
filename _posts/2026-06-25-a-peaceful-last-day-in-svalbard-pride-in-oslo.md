Our last full day of Svalbard ☹ It was a brisk but beautiful 46 degrees – very consistent while we have been here. The first day was extremely sunny but overcast the next few days. As our guide would say, it IS the arctic!

We had our bags all packed up and after breakfast we headed out to meet our boat tour of the fjord. This time we got front row seats on the boat. My phone was prepped with audiobooks and I had a new chip flavor to try 😊No seasickness this time!

It was pretty chilly at a couple points so I tried their cocoa - yum! It was nice to lean back and admire the deep blue/green hue of the water while relaxing to an audiobook. Currently listening to [“World Travel” by Anthony Bourdain](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/50496808-world-travel). Mike is listening to [Red Rising by Pierce Brown](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/15839976-red-rising) – a series he’s quickly devoured this year. They have a cool theatrical version where each character is played by a different actor and there are sound effects!

Our tour took us to enormous glacier! We saw all manner of birds and even more beluga whales! A really peaceful way to spend the day.

{% gallery_block %7B%22photos%22%3A%5B%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782673171%2F20260626_113406_bonllj.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782673169%2F20260626_113408_ci4nsu.jpg%22%2C%22https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdgw35sldo%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_limit%2Cf_auto%2Cq_auto%2Cw_1200%2Fv1782673170%2F20260626_113410_nvnur0.jpg%22%5D%2C%22caption%22%3A%22%22%7D %}

Our flight wasn’t until 9pm that evening so we held our bags in storage and went to read in a local coffee shop – ah, bliss  They even had my caramel iced coffee! Pretty glad we discovered this on the last day lol. The cakes and coffee were delightful and the hours flew by! I started [The Daisy Chain Flower Shop](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/236368418-the-daisy-chain-flower-shop) by Laurie Gilmore and its SO cute. The 6th book in a series and I’ve immediately read each one. All my favorite authors came out with books this year and I’ve already read each one ☹ When will I learn to slowly savor??

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782673288/20260626_150002_m8cgxh.jpg)

Dinner was a revisit to the Japanese restaurant where I had really good/flavorful ramen and Mike had a tuna poke bowl! (he says it was “fine”). I swear he does like things sometimes. 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782673329/20260626_175154_e9bbhl.jpg)

It was time! We grabbed our bags and headed to the tiny airport – it only has ONE gate! The flight was 2.5 hours long and I got to read and even nap! Landing, we hopped on the train to our Airbnb for two nights in Oslo before heading to our next landing spot. Norway had played in the World cup (and lost) this evening and the streets were LOUD and busy. Uh oh. 

Our Airbnb was right off the busy area and it was lively. We got in at 2am and people were still partying in the streets! And they had lost?!

Thankfully, we were so tired all we could do was shower and pass out.

Today we woke up starving and quickly found a spot near us that had breakfast (Mike even found one that had my coffee too!)

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782673395/20260627_092228_bjilfs.jpg)

I had a weird but good egg sandwich and my caramel iced coffee while Mike had a chocolate pastry and double Americano. Some areas of Europe do not have drip coffee, only espresso. 

That afternoon we found a massage spot and got relaxing, deep massages. Some parts were so painful I wanted to cry (in a good way) at another point, I was so relaxed that I fell asleep lol. What a roller coaster! A nice walk downtown led us back to the bookstore I had spotted several titles I’ve never seen before. They aren’t on Amazon or my Libby app :o. I have a few library cards on there but am always searching for more…If you want to share your library card with me...I won’t say no… Anyways! I bought a new book(hooray!) I am not going without a good paperback yet. Mixing it in nicely with my kindle books. I bought, “[Instructions for Heartbreak"](https://www.goodreads.com/en/book/show/214997861-instructions-for-heartbreak) by Sarah Handyside. 

Across the street was a smoothie shop where I got a blueberry protein shake and Mike got a strawberry one. Neither of us liked ours but we did like each other’s so we swapped! Lovely how nicely that works out sometimes 😊

Since the early morning, we had noticed a lot of pride flags and people decked out in pride gear! I know it’s pride month but is something happening today?? YES! There was a parade about to begin AND we were sitting along the route! Can you believe it?!

We grabbed our books and a blanket from our Airbnb and went down to the park to read – naturally, there was a gelato shop along the way so who am I to resist?? We beat the heat in the shade along the riverfront in the park for a few hours.

It was dinner time so we decided to honor our Mexican food tradition with Freddy Fuegos. It was not good. The guac was very good. But the rest was an insult to Mexican food everywhere. We took a nice walk after dinner where we spotted this random church tucked in-between a few shops as well as another park a block from our place!

We had knocked out laundry earlier in the day so we packed up for the early train the next morning. I finished my Daisy Chain book and started on Heartbreak Instructions, loving getting so much reading in with beautiful backgrounds!

It was still very warm in the apartment (no AC here) and VERY loud outside the apartment. People were still loudly out and about at 430am! We ended up sleeping hard, although I don’t know how!
