The last London bucket list item to attend to was having tea! We grabbed “Breakfast tea” and two blueberry muffins from a café called Café Piazza. Holy guacamole, best muffins we have ever consumed! They were so fluffy and the tea was so strong! 

Michael was able to secure us tickets to see Hamlet, the Shakespearian play at the Shakespeare Globe – Sam Wanamaker. Odd is the best way I can describe it. But entertaining. Act III was definitely worth staying for and the actors were superb!

We left by train at about 8am the next morning for Belgium. First, checking our EU Healthpasses, negative covid tests, and Passenger Locator Forms. Then we moved through an Airport-like security checkpoint and finally through customs where they checked our passports, Eu Healthpasses, and negative COVID tests again. WE GOT OUR FIRST PASSPORT STAMPS!!

With minimal people traveling during COVID, it wasn’t very full but it was very quiet! The train ride was two hours and we got to go through the underwater English Channel Tunnel as well as the English Countryside,  followed by the France and Belgium Countryside.
