The watch tower was just around the corner from the Imperial Palace which does not look like a palace at all to me! Some fun facts about this location are:

1) The courtyard stone is made of a softer material so if someone falls, they will not be seriously injured.

2) The Emperor appears on the terrace here twice a year to speak to the attendees (New Year and the Emperor's birthday.

3) The parking is all underground!
