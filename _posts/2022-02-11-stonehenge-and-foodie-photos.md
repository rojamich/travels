But Stonehenge was more breathtaking than I imagined and very peaceful. There were minimal tourists and it was another comfortable and overcast day. We couldn’t believe there had still not been any rain in London since our arrival! We really enjoyed learning about the history of Stonehenge and the possible explanations for why it the stones were placed where they were. 

We had a lot of fun seeing how many of us it would take to pull the stones the way they were theorized to have moved!
