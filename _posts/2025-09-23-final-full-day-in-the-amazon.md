We had time before dinner to enjoy paddleboarding! I had been eyeing the paddleboards since we arrived, and we finally had time! I love paddleboarding (not in the ocean though), so this was perfect! Mike killed it on this with his balance!

  


That evening, we enjoyed a night canoeing down the Amazon River. We just listened to the sounds of the jungle. With only the light from the moon to guide us, we were able to hear monkeys, birds, and the sound of the river. The fireflies were dancing all over the riverbanks, and it was so cool to see them!!
