We returned to Jack's cafe in Cusco that morning to soak up one last fantastic breakfast as we did not know what kind of food the Amazon would have to offer, haha. It did not disappoint! The flavors make my mouth water just thinking of it. Mike showed me that his dish looked like a face haha! I grabbed a coffee to go and we headed back for our hotel to pack up and head to the airport.

  


It was a short flight from Cusco back to Lima and then Lima to Iquitos, the town we would depart from, into the Amazon. Iquitos is not known to be incredibly safe, so we got a certified taxi from the airport, directly to our hotel. Our lodge for the Amazon had left us instructions and a gift of two parrot keychains, A promise of things to come :) Holy Moly, it was humid in this town! I checked the weather, and it had 95% humidity.

Mike and I decided to drop our stuff and head to the gym. It was small but had what we needed. We then turned in for the night after a yummy dinner. Outside our window, the city was still bustling with tuk-tuks going by nonstop! The city is absolutely filled with tuk-tuks and motorcycles, stuffed three people deep. There were people in the park across the street watching a man dance like Michael Jackson to his music on a Bluetooth speaker as well!
