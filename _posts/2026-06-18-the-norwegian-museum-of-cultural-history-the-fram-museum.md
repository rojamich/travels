Another early wake up with the sun high in the sky at 5am! I woke up at 3am to pouring rain which lulled me back to sleep, how perfect :)

I decided to head down for a dance warm-up and then run through a big park nearby. We are on the top floor so every day has stairs to be conquered. This city is immensely green which was unexpected. I pictured a grey city like most of Germanys. 

I managed another nap after my run and we both got over 8 hours of sleep again, hooray! That means we have both almost overcome all the sleep debt we have accumulated from the last month of craziness! Thanks Norway :)

Breakfast and out the door for a day filled with museums! I snagged a cookie from the bakery a block away (that's so dangerous lol) and we headed to the bus stop. That's right, we have officially taken the same amount of public transportation in two days of Norway as we did our whole lives in the US! Insane!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1781888435/20260619_110712_kk1dea.jpg "You get to see a few things you may not be accustomed to seeing in every day life, like a restaurant called the Bastard Burgers lol")

Our first museum was the Norwegian Museum of Cultural History - an open air museum filled with 160 buildings from the 1600-1800s that were moved and rebuilt here for preservation and viewing!
