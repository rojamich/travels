After a 330am wake up, we made the drive up to Auckland for our flight down to Queenstown, in the south island of New Zealand. The flight was a quick 1.5 hour and the Queenstown airport was far superior to the Auckland airport. The view during the descent was fantastic!

We were not prepared for the cold! Even more so here in the south island! The first photo is filled with glaciers!

  


Something interesting about New Zealand is that the pedestrians must yield to cars- not the other way around!

  


Queenstown is very updated and a resort town. Lots of winter sport options as well as water sports!
