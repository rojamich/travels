The building was made of the most expensive material at the time, marble. And it came from over 60 miles away...by boat! Milan, completely landlocked, used to be one of Italy's major ports! They dug canals all throughout the city and didn't fill them in until the 20th century.  
  
These two buildings to the right of the Duomo were built to be twin buildings. Mussolini gave speeches from these very balconies.
