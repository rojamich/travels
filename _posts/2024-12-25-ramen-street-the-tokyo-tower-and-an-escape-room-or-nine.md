Let's talk crosswalks! Here are what the symbols look like for walk, don't walk. The little hashes on the sides indicate how long you have left to wait or cross. There is no jaywalking here. We have only seen one person (after a week of walking with hundreds of people) jaywalk! This was very reminiscent of Germany for us and, I have to say, I'm proud of us tourists for collectively adhering to these rules. We haven't seen litter either but that could be due to cleaners (unsure).
