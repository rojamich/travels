We were on the road again! Definitely thought we would need more bags for all the shopping we wanted to do here! The dollar-to-yen conversion is very much in our favor right now. 

  


The next leg of the trip was much anticipated as we were on our way to Lake Kawaguchi which is near Mt. Fuji! We ran ourselves ragged during this trip and were absolutely looking forward to our relaxing stay!

  


Tokyo was fantastic and an amazing city with little pockets of mini cultures - every town had a different feel and style. 

It also had a crap ton of walking. 

Total distance walked: 71 miles!

  


It was time for another train adventure and I was all too excited. My Kindle is loaded with cozy mystery novels and we are loaded down with 7/11 snacks. Aka we are prepped!
