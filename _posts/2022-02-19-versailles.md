We woke bright and early the next morning to try and catch the first train to Versailles!

Our neighbors in San Diego had referred us to the Rick Steves podcast which gives you free audio tours to museums, sights and foods all over Europe! We were excited to use this resource at the Palace!

We were about 20th in line at the Palace, which has famously long lines!

The app was _so_ helpful and insightful! We learned a lot about the French Revolution and the tour explained everything we were looking at! It was interesting to hear about the formal galas held there and every King that had resided at the (originally) hunting lodge !

I had to take a moment to brace and collect myself when I saw this staggering fireplace! #Fireplacegoals

The palace was much more than we ever expected and the audio guide was captivating and informative! Most people raced through it but the tour allowed us to really get into the experience. I don’t think we would have appreciated the rich history nearly as much without it.
