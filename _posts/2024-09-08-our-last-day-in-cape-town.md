After a good night's rest I was determined to make the most out of the hotel amenities. A few times during our stay I enjoyed the conference room space in our hotel room (usually before 6am so no one was around) and practiced my dancing! If you don't know, I've been linedancing weekly since February! I don't want to forget the steps to the songs I know so when we travel, I usually walk around in search of a space big enough to secretly dance in. One morning a security guard and I scared the crap out of each other lol.

Anyways! I practiced my dancing, got a run in on the treadmill and a workout before we hit the town in search of a good breakfast spot.

This place was called "nice". Holy moly it was so good. The cheese was so gooey and we ordered something called a Crookie which is chocolate chip cookies surrounding a croissant with nutella and powdered sugar (served hot). Holy lord it was divine! We vowed to return the next morning for our last meal in Cape Town.    
  
Next on our schedule was finding a laundry spot (so we had all clean clothes for our next stay) and I had a nail appointment! We typically do laundry in the hotel room and hang out the clothes on a travel laundry line but we would not have time for everything to dry and needed a full bag of clean clothes before heading to the overnight safari in Kruger National Park!

We wanted to highlight how lucky we got weather wise on this trip! This is Table Mountain with what the locals call it's "tablecloth"! It many times is covered in fog and we lucked out on the days we hiked to have beautiful sunny days! When we planned this trip, we saw the rain typically ends on August 30th and we are grateful this was true during our trip.

I've been really eager to try Mochi and there was a spot near our hotel I'd been eyeing since our arrival in Cape Town. We went in and picked out a few to try! Mike tried a savory cheese bun and I picked out rose, salted caramel, milk tart, and turkish delight!

  
I'd rate them:

  
Milk tart  
Caramel  
Turkish  
  
  
  
  
rose.  
The texture was wonderful and they were delicious!   
  
Black Betty's had promised to call their other location to see if there was my size available in the ring we found but we had not heard from them by noon and it was near our hotel so we stopped by on a whim. The woman there instinctually asked the creators upstairs if they could fit in a same-day ring re-size and they promised to try. We had done our research as well to ensure this business deals only in non-conflict jewelry. If you didn't notice, they actually have their workshop just above their ring shop and you can hear them actually making the rings above you! The creator came down to ensure my size as well which was neat!   
  
A few hours later we came back at the agreed upon time to see if the resize had been possible and OMG it was and I love it!!
