Today I was highly looking forward to Mike's appointment at [Barbaard ](https://barbaard.com/)- a Dutch-style barber experience!

But first! Hanoi's train street. If you've ever seen Vietnam on Instagram, two things might have been in the post. Ha Long Bay and/or Hanoi's train street. The streets are packed today because it's New Years Eve! We saw performers practicing and preparing for their upcoming performances!

The special part of this street is that the train runs right down the middle of a typically pedestrian-filled walkway! Shops line the train tracks and you can sit mere feet away from the train crossing and enjoy a (very expensive) meal or beverage!
