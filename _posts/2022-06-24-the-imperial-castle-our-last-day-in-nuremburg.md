I really liked Nuremburg! It was a pretty city with lots of food and shopping options. The people there were really kind and understanding for the most part. We actually had to wake up a bit earlier the next morning to start the train ride out to Berlin, our last city in Germany ☹ I had a lot of fun with Mike here watching our show and enjoying our food exploration and dreaming of cooking fresh pasta together.
