Our bodies aren't as tragically sore as we imagined they would be this morning! Hooray! At least all that freezing water was good for something 🤣

We made our breakfast and packed up for the day ahead, stopping to read and play games as we had some time before check out. 

There was a nearby cafe and we parked there and scouted the place. Are there cookies? Coffee? A workspace? Yes. Yes. and YES! Mike locked up parking and I searched for an open booth. A group appeared to be leaving so I asked and the gentleman said yes! He seemed very excited! He then said, "Your English is SO good! Where are you from?!

I nearly choked on my own spit before getting out that I'm from Oregon - it was too dang funny! I didn't outwardly laugh, only on the inside. We chatted about where he's from (Michigan) and hiking before his group took off after clearly the table. I may never forget that interaction. I'm so proud of my English 😮

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783798921/20260709_110102_vtvlzw.jpg)

A hot caramel latte and chocolate chip cookie for me (yum on both counts) and an Americano and custard pastry for Mike (also good!) What a score! We worked there for the next hour before the wifi slowed down and we called it a wrap. 

There are very few food spots on the archipelago so we grabbed sushi before taking off for our next town! The gentleman at the front recognized us and said, 

> "Welcome back! I hope you enjoyed it yesterday, or a couple days ago? 
>
> I feigned innocence and said, "I'm not sure what you mean, sir! I've never been here" (This was with my jokey face on).
>
> He looked uncertain but also jokey and then got serious..."but really, it was you guys right?"
>
> "Oh! Yes! It was, sorry! haha!"
>
> Him: "Oh good, I would feel bad if I was wrong."
>
> I had a feeling everyone recognizes Mike by the beard, not many places lately have had facial hair! 
>
> So I asked, "Was it the beard?" Motioning to Mike.
>
> He said, "No, you both are just very attractive!"

Well ok then 🤣🤣 There isn't even tipping here!

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1783799535/20260709_124910_r35rei.jpg)

Feeling fortified, it was finally time for my first manual driving lesson.
