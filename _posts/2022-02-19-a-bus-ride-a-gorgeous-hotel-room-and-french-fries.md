Our train from Belgium to France was cancelled so the only alternative in our price range was a bus. After a miserable four-hour bus ride from Belgium to France, we finally made it! Watching the French countryside pass by was relaxing at least. 

We took a metro to the room where we were greeted with our upgrade! Mike requested on through the Westin about 6 weeks ago and it paid off! A view of the Eiffel Tour from the room in the Junior Presidential Suite! It was absolutely breath-taking and magnificent. It was hard to leave the room! After having two shared Airbnbs, it was nice not to have to whisper or tip toe around. The whole suite was ours!

 Mike also arranged for a HUGE Bouquet of red roses, rose petals lining our entrance, macarons, and a love note (plus they threw in a €180 bottle of champagne)! It was so sweet omg. That view was to die for! The bed was ridiculously comfortable with a million pillows.
