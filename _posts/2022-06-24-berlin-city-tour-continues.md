We took a break to warm up in a nearby Starbucks with a view of the Brandenburg gate. The Goddess who tops it was once referred to as the Goddess of Peace but later renamed as the Goddess of Victory. We had thought the wall stood on its own as an entry marker to the city (much like the Arc de Triumph in Paris or the one in Rome) but it doesn’t look as imposing with the buildings beside it.
