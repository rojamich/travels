We had an easy morning before setting off for our kayaking tour today! Still no flavored coffee in Norway so I'm really kicking the habit here lol. Thanks Norway!

Our guides picked us up and took us to their office where we tried on our dry suits, booties, and life-jackets. 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782323645/20260624_104536_xg892p.jpg "Ps. It's really hard to get your head through the head-hole. ")

Bussed to the boats, we climbed aboard and started the journey across the Fjord to the kayaks! 

![](https://res.cloudinary.com/dgw35sldo/image/upload/c_limit,f_auto,q_auto,w_1200/v1782323687/GOPR0124_td8gms.jpg)

On the way there, we spotted beluga whales! First time ever! P.s. Beluga whales are exclusively found in the cold, seasonally ice-covered coastal waters of the Arctic and sub-Arctic regions.
