We were initially worried about our hotel location but ended up loving it! Directly across from the safest train station ever. No homeless, no scammers, and super clean! They even had two Starbucks! We stayed in the Aloft Munich, it was so comfortable and we were glad to be in a hotel for a bit. 

The guy at the front desk was funny and so helpful. We got a few masks to change out our old ones since it was mandatory to wear the FFP2s there (same as Italy).  

It was quite a bit colder than we had gotten used to (after being in Italy for 10 days). It was in the low 20s in the morning and high of 42 during the day. So, it was quite a shock getting in the first day.
