The hike was definitely worth the views. And let me just reaffirm my belief in extremely comfortable walking shoes while we are on the topic of walking. Almost everywhere we have been has had insane “pavement” consisting of paving stones that were uneven and huge spaces between them. I had the best boots I own, on, and these stones still made me roll my ankles!
