Our paddleboard trip was in the city of Vassena so Mike mapped out our route for the day and we set off. We caught the first ferry of the day (nearly empty) from Lierna to Vassena with one quick stop between. This landed us in Vassena an hour earlier than our appointment time for our paddleboarding but we thought, what the heck, let's see if we can go early.

It both reminded us of New Zealand and got me even more eager to one day visit Norway. SUP in Norway is a huge bucket-list item for me. 

An hour flew by and we were back on shore. Despite some arm soreness, I probably could've gone another hour happily. We were so glad to have chosen to go in the morning! We were initially worried it would be really cold! But somehow, it was the perfect temperature. We also realized how bad the waves would be once all the private boats and ferries were up and running! Whew! Glad we dodged that bullet! 

We had some time before the next ferry and decided to look around Vassena, it was really tiny! But Mike found a post office so I could send off the last two postcards to Mom and Dad. In the last two months before our trip, my dad kept telling people we would be going away for 6 months or more... I'm like Dad. It's 6 weeks. He's like who knows if you'll ever come back. Oh, dad. He jokingly (or not) said to send postcards. So I did. From every country we visited!

There was a cafe down by the water and we decided to sit and wait, get a few snacks for the ferry, and Mike have an espresso. We sat and took in the view. There was a precious little girl next to us speaking German so we recognized some words which was cool!

The waitress brought Mike's coffee and excitedly said, Do you know you look JUST like Jennifer Gardener?! I said "Oh my gosh thank you! I think she is so beautiful!"

Mike says I turned tomato red and then the waitress turned tomato red and said, "Yes, you are so beautiful!" Good golly I'll be riding that high for weeks!

AND, in Florence last year, a woman told Mike and me we look like Ben Affleck and Jennifer Gardener so let's just add that to the reasons why we love Italy. 

It was time for the ferry and we watched a woman get shouted at for being on the dock before the ferry arrived- we were about to walk down as well so ...whew close one. We took the ferry to Bellagio which is a very upscale seeming city on Lake Como.

Two ferries pulled in at the same time and FLOODED the entire area with tourists. The restaurants closest to the shore didn't really look like the food we were looking for so we tried searching the ratings of different restaurants. Unfortunately, we tend not to agree with most restaurant ratings in Europe so we just decided to wing it! 

We found a pasta place off the beaten path and I finally got one of my favorite pasta: gnocchi. Mike got ravioli and we split both. DAMNED good. If I close my eyes, I can still taste the black truffle sauce in Mike's and feel the creamy texture of mine!
