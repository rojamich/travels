Mike did really well on this one especially finding the clue inside the heel of a boot!

I kept seeing these random bears everywhere so Mike looked up where they came from. The city of Berlin is historically a place of murder and destruction. Turning a new leaf, the city wanted to add some color and positivity to their image; resulting in the decorative bear statues!

The next morning we saw the DDR museum which was really neat and showed us what life looked like for East Germans. Mike and I got into a Traavi which was the car of East Germans. They had to manufacture them with plastic and it took 18 months to get your car once you purchased it. They smuggled quite a few people over the border in this vehicle.
